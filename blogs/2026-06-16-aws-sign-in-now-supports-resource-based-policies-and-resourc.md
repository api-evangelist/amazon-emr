AWS Sign-in now supports resource-based policies and resource control policies (RCPs) for the AWS Management Console, letting you restrict console sign-in to expected networks. Policies are evaluated during sign-in and when sessions request new credentials, available at no additional cost in all AWS commercial Regions.
