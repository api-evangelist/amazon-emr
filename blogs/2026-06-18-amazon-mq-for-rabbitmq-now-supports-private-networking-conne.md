Amazon MQ for RabbitMQ now supports private networking, enabling brokers to connect to private resources in your VPC without exposing those resources publicly. This helps meet security and compliance requirements when brokers need to reach private identity providers such as LDAP and OAuth 2.0, other Amazon MQ brokers, or self-hosted RabbitMQ brokers.
