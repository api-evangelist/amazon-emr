AWS announces the availability of bmn-cx3a instances on second-generation AWS Outposts racks, featuring 5th Gen AMD EPYC processors with a maximum frequency of 4.1 GHz and NVIDIA ConnectX-7 network interface cards. They deliver up to 800 Gbps of bare-metal accelerated network bandwidth operating at near line rate.
