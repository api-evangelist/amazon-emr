AWS Graviton5-based M9g database instances are now generally available for Amazon RDS for PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MariaDB. Graviton5-based instances provide up to a 30% performance improvement and up to a 23% price/performance improvement over Graviton4-based instances of equivalent sizes, depending on engine, version, and workload.
