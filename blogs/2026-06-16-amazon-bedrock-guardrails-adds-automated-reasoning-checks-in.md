Automated Reasoning checks in Amazon Bedrock Guardrails use formal verification techniques to validate AI model outputs with up to 99% accuracy in detecting correct responses, addressing hallucinations and policy compliance for regulated industries. The capability is now available in the Asia Pacific (Sydney) Region, joining US East, US West, and several Europe Regions.
