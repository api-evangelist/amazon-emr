Amazon Redshift is expanding general availability of Graviton-based RG instances to Africa (Cape Town), Asia Pacific (Bangkok), and Mexico (Central), delivering up to 4.2X better price-performance and running up to 2.4x faster than previous-generation RA3 instances at 30% less per vCPU. Customers can provision rg.xlarge and rg.4xlarge node types.
