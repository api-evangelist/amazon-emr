title: "Amazon MSK Express brokers now support Intelligent Rebalancing on existing clusters"

date: "2026-06-18"
feed_url: "https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/recent/feed/"
---
Amazon MSK Provisioned clusters with Express brokers now support Intelligent Rebalancing on all existing clusters at no additional cost, previously available only on newly created clusters. This makes it effortless for customers to benefit from automatic partition balancing when scaling Express-based clusters up or down.
