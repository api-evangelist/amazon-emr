AWS Compute Optimizer now includes improved visibility into IOPS and throughput spikes when delivering Amazon EBS volume rightsizing recommendations. It analyzes two additional CloudWatch metrics, VolumeIOPSExceededCheck and VolumeThroughputExceededCheck, which report whether workloads consistently attempted to exceed provisioned performance in any given minute.
