Nested virtualization is now available on additional Intel platforms and additional Regions. It is now supported on C7i, R7i, M7i, C8id, R8id, M8id, C7i-flex, M7i-flex, I7i, C8i-flex, R8i-flex, M8i-flex, and X8i, in addition to the already available C8i, M8i, and R8i instances.
