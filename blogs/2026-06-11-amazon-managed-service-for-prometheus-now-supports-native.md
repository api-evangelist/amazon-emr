Amazon Managed Service for Prometheus now supports ingestion, storage, and querying of Prometheus native histograms, enabling customers to capture high-resolution metric distributions with greater precision and lower cardinality than classic histograms. DevOps engineers, site reliability engineers, and platform teams monitoring latency, request durations, and other distributions can now get more accurate percentile calculations without pre-defining bucket boundaries or managing high-cardinality time series.
